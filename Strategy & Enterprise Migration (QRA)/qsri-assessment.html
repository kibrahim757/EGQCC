About QSRI

Purpose

Quantify how well an organization is prepared for quantum threats. Provide standardized benchmarking for internal assessment, industry comparison, and regulatory reporting.

Alignment

Supports risk management, migration roadmaps, and investment prioritization. Aligns with NIST PQC, ETSI, and ENISA guidance.

Methodology

Eight critical dimensions weighted by strategic importance. Maturity levels 0-5 assessed per dimension with weighted scoring 0-100.
